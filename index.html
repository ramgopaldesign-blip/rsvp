About Event
Join fellow developers and tech enthusiasts for the Ruby SG November Meetup. An evening of learning, sharing, and community connection. Expect lightning talks, project demos, and discussions around the latest Ruby and Rails updates.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, this is a great opportunity to network with the local Ruby community and exchange insights on real-world challenges, best practices, and new updates.

What to Expect:
The evening will kick off with networking over refreshments, giving you a chance to connect with fellow Ruby enthusiasts before the talks begin. We'll have three lightning talks covering topics from Rails 7.1 features, performance optimization techniques, and real-world case studies from local startups.

Schedule:
6:30 PM - Doors open, networking and refreshments
7:00 PM - Welcome and introductions
7:15 PM - Lightning Talk 1: "What's New in Rails 7.1"
7:35 PM - Lightning Talk 2: "Performance Optimization Tips"
7:55 PM - Break
8:10 PM - Lightning Talk 3: "Building Scalable APIs with Ruby"
8:30 PM - Open discussion and Q&A
9:00 PM - Wrap up and continued networking

Who Should Attend:
This meetup is perfect for Ruby developers of all levels, from beginners to experts. Whether you're working with Rails daily, exploring Ruby for side projects, or considering it for your next application, you'll find value in the discussions and connections made here.

Topics We'll Cover:
• Latest Ruby and Rails updates and features
• Performance optimization strategies
• Testing best practices
• Deployment and DevOps considerations
• Community projects and open source contributions
• Career development and learning resources

Why Attend:
Singapore's Ruby community is vibrant and welcoming. This meetup provides a unique opportunity to learn from experienced developers, share your own experiences, and build lasting professional relationships. Many attendees have found job opportunities, collaboration partners, and mentors through our meetups.

We encourage everyone to participate in discussions, ask questions, and share their perspectives. Don't be shy – even if you're new to Ruby, your fresh perspective is valuable to the community.

Location & Venue:
The venue will be announced to confirmed attendees. It's easily accessible by public transport and will have ample space for networking and presentations.

Food & Drinks:
Light refreshments and beverages will be provided. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know in advance.

Looking forward to seeing you there!
